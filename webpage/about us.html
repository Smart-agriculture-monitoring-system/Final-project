A smart agriculture monitoring system is a comprehensive technology that includes sensors for soil and DHT11 to monitor environmental conditions such as humidity and temperature. It also incorporates a water pump with both automatic and manual modes to regulate soil moisture levels. The system is connected to an application that can be accessed through the cloud Firebase platform. Through the application, farmers can monitor crop conditions, control the water pump, and receive alerts if soil moisture levels fall below or exceed certain thresholds. Overall, the smart agriculture monitoring system enables farmers to make data-driven decisions that optimize crop growth and yield




About

A smart agriculture monitoring system is a cutting-edge solution that provides farmers with a range of advanced features and technologies to help them optimize their crops. this system leverages real-time data and advanced analytics to help farmers monitor soil moisture levels, detect potential issues with their crops and predict which crops to plant based on the season. Additionally, the system includes a powerful simulation tool that can help farmers determine the optimal quantity of fertilizers, pesticides, and insecticides to use. By providing farmers with precise recommendations, the system helps them reduce waste and save money while still achieving high yields. this system is a powerful tool for modern farmers looking to improve their farming pactices.
